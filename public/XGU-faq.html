What is XGU crypto web wallet?

XGU crypto web wallet is a browser based mobile/web app that allows you to manage all of your cryptocurrency wallets and cryptocurrency assets in one place, offering a friendly and intuitive UI.

Features

  • You maintain full control your private keys
  • It's fully anonymous
  • It's open source
  • Hierarchical Deterministic
  • Web based
  • Mobile optimized
  • Manage multiple cryptocurrency assets
  • Easy to use

Our vision

We believe in a decentralized future where everybody will use cryptocurrencies with decentralized apps. XGU crypto web wallet aims to offer you the best decentralized wallet, enabling you to manage all your assets.

We will never store any of your data. XGU crypto web wallet does not use any database and is fully browser based.

We don't have a login nor will we ever ask you for an email.

In sum, XGU crypto web wallet doesn't know anything about you. The only data we collect is via Google Analytics, tracking the behavior of the users. This data is solely used to help us improve the XGU crypto web wallet application to the benefit of our users.

Is it secure?

Yes, XGU crypto web wallet is secure. We will never store any private key, Private keys are always encrypted in the browser.

Even if your computer is compromised your private key cannot be accessed because they are encrypted and we only allocate them temporarily in browser-memory.

Any time you need to transact a coin, you will need to type your password to decrypt your private key. While this may seem tedious at first, we understand the importance of security above all else when managing your digital assets.

Other wallets use the same password for all of your assets. XGU crypto web wallet provides you with the possibility of setting a different password for each asset. Our recommendation is for you to use different passwords.

Why use XGU crypto web wallet?

Each cryptocurrency wallet has its pros and cons depending on what you need. Here's a short comparative overview between XGU crypto web wallet and other wallets publicly available:

Why choose XGU crypto web wallet over a desktop wallet such a Exodus, Electrum or Jaxx?

You only need a modern browser when using XGU crypto web wallet. There are no downloads or updates required. XGU crypto web wallet lets you have instant access to your wallets directly on your mobile devices, laptop and/or desktop.

Why choose XGU crypto web wallet over another browser wallet such a blockchain.info, MyEtherWallet or bitaddress.org?

Blockchain.info asks you for an email, and they store your private key for you. MyEtherWallet or bitaddress are more similar to XGU crypto web wallet as they are browser-based and they do not store any of your data. However, they only support Ethereum and Bitcoin respectively. XGU crypto web wallet aims to support as many types of cryptocurrencies as possible.

Why choose XGU crypto web wallet over a hardware wallet such a Trezor or Ledger nano?

The main reason is basically that XGU crypto web wallet is free.

But hardware wallets are much more secure right?

Yes, they are. If you lose your gadget you may always restore your wallets using your 12/24 words seed. XGU crypto web wallet operates in the same way: you must print paper-wallets or write down your 12 words each time you create a new asset.

Are there tradeoffs to using XGU crypto web wallet?

Yes, there are tradeoffs. As we don't store any of your data, it is your sole responsibility to keep your paper wallets or your 12 words seed safe. If you lose them, we are not able to restore your assets.

Currently, we are working on a user interface that lets you save your data properly securely.
